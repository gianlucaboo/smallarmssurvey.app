A Whole of System Approach to C-IED

A whole of system approach to C-IED refers to an approach by all C-IED stakeholders including multiple elements of state security, defence, government departments and agencies and civil society organisations often with complex institutional structures and procedures.
These could be considered as the internal state elements involved in the C-IED enterprise, with external elements involving regional and international organisations also potentially involved.
