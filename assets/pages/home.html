A Systemic Approach to Counter Improvised Explosive Devices (C-IED)

According to Dr. Sani Adamu, Programmes Officer, Disarmament and Arms Control, ECOWAS, who is involved in regional C-IED efforts in West Africa, “before you counter a problem, you need to know the problem.”

In the case of C-IED this refers to understanding the use or threatened use of IEDs, so that in time effective and efficient C-IED efforts may be invested in as part of a C-IED enterprise to at least match but ideally overmatch the IED threat.

This website aims at providing a comprehensive yet accessible overview of the main components of the IED system and how a whole of system approach to C-IED can contribute to neutralizing the IED threat.
