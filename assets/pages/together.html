Maintaining an Accurate IED Threat Picture

A national C-IED enterprise is often engaged in an action-reaction-counteraction cycle with an IED system. It is for this reason, why maintaining an updated IED threat picture is essential and requires on-going timely information sharing between all C-IED stakeholders.
Information sharing between C-IED stakeholders is a key enabler to success in efforts to at least match but ideally overmatch the threat posed.

Oftentimes communication is hampered by the use of inconsistent and often unnecessarily technically complex language for the target audiences being communicated with.
Despite the somewhat technical nature of C-IED, this may be addressed using clear, precise, and uncomplicated language, so that all stakeholders understand the threat as well as the C-IED efforts that collectively make up the C-IED enterprise being invested in.
